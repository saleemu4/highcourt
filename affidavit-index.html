Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our affidavit services.

What is an affidavit? +
An affidavit is a written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation for use as evidence in court or other legal proceedings. It is a legally binding document where a person swears that the facts contained in the document are true to the best of their knowledge.
How long does it take to process an affidavit? +
The processing time for an affidavit typically takes 1-3 business days once all required information and payment have been received. Complex cases may take longer. You can track the status of your affidavit request through your account dashboard.
What documents do I need to provide? +
The required documents vary depending on the type of affidavit you're requesting. Generally, you will need to provide your valid ID (National ID, driver's license, or passport), recent passport photograph, and any supporting documents relevant to your affidavit (such as birth certificates for age declaration, or documents showing previous name for change of name). The system will guide you through the specific requirements during the application process.
How much does an affidavit cost? +
The fee for an affidavit depends on the type. Standard affidavits cost between ‚Ç¶2,000 and ‚Ç¶5,000. More complex affidavits may have additional fees. The exact cost will be displayed before you confirm your request and proceed to payment.
Can I apply for an affidavit on behalf of someone else? +
Yes, you can apply for an affidavit on behalf of someone else. However, you will need to provide additional documentation proving your relationship to the person and/or authorization to act on their behalf. The system will guide you through the required documentation during the application process.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept various payment methods including debit/credit cards, bank transfers, and popular mobile payment platforms like Paystack, Flutterwave, and other local payment options. All payments are processed securely through our payment gateway.
